## Priority Definitions

| Priority | Meaning | MVP |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **P0** | Must-have — MVP cannot launch without it | ✅ Required |
| **P1** | Should-have — important but deferrable if schedule slips | ⚠️ Target MVP |
| **P2** | Nice-to-have — post-MVP or fast-follow | ❌ Deferred |
